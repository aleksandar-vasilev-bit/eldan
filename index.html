| PHILOSOPHY

Eldan Construction, with long-term experience in photovoltaic systems, specializes in the construction of support systems covering the requirements of each project.

Combining innovative design, automated production process, and top-quality materials with Magnelis® coating technology, we guarantee high construction quality, faster delivery and long-term performance of your investment.

Magnelis® is a pioneering discovery in corrosion protection, which ensures corrosion resistance significantly higher than conventional galvanizing and similar to aluminum.

Our products are distinguished for their flexibility and adaptability to various terrains, climates and project specifications.
