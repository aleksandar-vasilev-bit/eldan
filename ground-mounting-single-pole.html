Product Description

The Single Pole ground mounting system is engineered for optimal performance in utility-scale and commercial solar installations. This innovative solution combines cutting-edge design with robust construction to deliver unparalleled reliability and efficiency. The system features a streamlined installation process that significantly reduces labor costs and project timelines.

Our Single Pole system is designed to withstand extreme weather conditions, including high wind loads and heavy snow accumulation. The advanced Magnelis® coating technology ensures long-term corrosion resistance, making it ideal for coastal and humid environments. The system's modular design allows for easy scalability and maintenance throughout the project lifecycle.

With compatibility for both monofacial and bifacial panels, this mounting solution offers maximum flexibility for various project requirements. The optimized 25° tilt angle ensures peak energy production throughout the year, while the vertical panel orientation maximizes land usage efficiency.
