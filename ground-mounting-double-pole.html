Product Description

The Double Pole ground mounting system represents the pinnacle of structural engineering for large-scale photovoltaic installations. This robust solution is specifically designed for projects requiring enhanced stability and support for multiple panel configurations. The dual-support architecture distributes loads evenly, ensuring optimal structural integrity even in challenging terrain conditions.

Featuring premium S350GD steel construction with Magnelis® corrosion protection, this system guarantees decades of maintenance-free operation. The innovative design accommodates various panel orientations and configurations, providing unmatched versatility for complex project layouts. Installation is simplified through our pre-engineered components and comprehensive assembly documentation.

The Two-Pole system excels in areas with high wind exposure and variable ground conditions. Its adjustable mounting brackets allow for precise panel alignment and easy maintenance access. The system's design has been validated through rigorous testing and complies with international structural standards, ensuring reliability and safety throughout its operational lifetime.
