Product Description

The Bifacial System is specifically engineered to maximize energy harvest from advanced bifacial solar panels. This cutting-edge mounting solution optimizes both direct sunlight capture and ground-reflected albedo radiation, resulting in energy yields up to 30% higher than traditional monofacial installations. The system's elevated design ensures optimal ground clearance for maximum rear-side irradiance.

Constructed with high-grade S350GD steel and protected by Magnelis® coating, this system offers exceptional durability in all environmental conditions. The adjustable tilt angle range (15-30°) allows for site-specific optimization, ensuring peak performance across different latitudes and seasonal variations. The modular design supports flexible panel configurations, from single to double-row arrangements.

Our Bifacial System incorporates advanced structural analysis to minimize shading effects while maintaining superior stability. The streamlined installation process reduces project costs and timelines, while the low-maintenance design ensures long-term operational efficiency. Comprehensive static calculations and TÜV certification guarantee compliance with the highest international standards.
