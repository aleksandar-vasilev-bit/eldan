Product Description

The Tile Roof mounting system is specifically designed for residential and commercial installations on traditional tile roofing. This specialized solution ensures secure panel attachment while maintaining the integrity and waterproofing of the existing roof structure. Our innovative hook design accommodates various tile profiles and dimensions, providing universal compatibility across different roofing styles.

Manufactured from premium S350GD steel with MagnelisÂ® corrosion protection, this system guarantees long-term reliability in all weather conditions. The installation process is carefully engineered to prevent roof damage and maintain proper drainage. Our rail system provides flexibility in panel layout while ensuring optimal structural support and wind resistance.

The system includes integrated flashing components and waterproof seals to prevent moisture intrusion. Adjustable mounting brackets allow for precise panel alignment on varying roof slopes, while the low-profile design maintains aesthetic appeal. Complete installation documentation and technical support ensure professional results on every project.
